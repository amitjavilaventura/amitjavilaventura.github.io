Computational Biology

Computational Biology, Universitat Oberta de Catalunya

March 2021 - Present
Master’s thesis student

In the context of my master’s thesis (MSc in Bioinformatics & Biostatsistics), and under the suprevision of Dr. Izaskun Mallona, I decided to unravel the ignorome of colon cancer. The ignorome, defined by Pandey et al, consists in the genes that can be important in the biological context of a tissue or disease, but that haven’t been well studied or related to that context because of publication biases or due to other reasons (see Stoeger et al).
