Epigenetics & Bioinformatics

Pasini’s lab, Istituto Europeo di Oncologia

July 2019 - July 2021
Bioinformatician

During my stage in Dr. Diego Pasini’s lab, I am investigating several molecular signaling pathways and epigenetic mechanisms underlying cellular differentiation and oncogenesis.

My contributions to this research were:
  • Comparison of different ATAC-seq peak callers.
  • Comparison of different sequencing depths in RNA-seq.
  • Development of an ATAC-seq pipeline to run using Snakemake. The code can be visualized here (still ongoing).
  • Development of a ShinyApp to allow wetlab members to visualize the omics produced in the lab in an easy way. The code can be visualized here (still ongoing).
  • Analysis of bulk RNA-seq, ChIP-seq and ATAC-seq.
