  Ex-elite rower in Club Natació Banyoles

Started rowing in 2007

2016 World Rowing Under-23 Champion in BLM2x

One of the most relevant components in my life has been sport, specially rowing, and here I will show some the results I had in sport

Rowing

I started rowing at the end of 2007 and, since then, I have trained and competed at my highest level to achieve my goals in this sport.

Unfortunatelly, I missed the qualification for the 2016 Olympic Games by one place. However, toghether with my crewmate, I became World Rowing Under-23 champion in 2016 in the category of Under-23 Lightweight Double Scull (BLM2x).

Finally I left the high-level competition in 2019, just before starting my PhD.
