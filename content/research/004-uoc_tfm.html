Computational Biology, Universitat Oberta de Catalunya

March 2021 - Present
Master’s thesis student

In the context of my master’s thesis (MSc in Bioinformatics & Biostatsistics), and under the suprevision of Dr. Izaskun Mallona, I decided to study the ignorome of colon/colorectal cancer. The ignorome, defined by Pandey et al, consists in the genes that can be important in the biological context of a tissue or disease, but that haven’t been well studied or related to that context because of publication biases or due to other reasons (see Stoeger et al).

My contributions to this research were:
  • Analysis of microarray expression data from cancerous and normal colon samples (data from GEO).
  • Analysis of RNA-seq data from TCGA (COAD/COREAD).
