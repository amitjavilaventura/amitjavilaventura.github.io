Pasini’s lab, Istituto Europeo di Oncologia

July 2019 - Present
Bioinformatician and PhD candidate

In the context of my PhD, under the direct supervision of Dr. Diego Pasini and together with Dr. Daria Manganaro, I started my project with the aim of dissecting the role of the Wnt/βcatenin pathway in colorectal cancer initiation and progression.

My contribution to this research was:
  • Comparison of different ATACseq peak callers.
  • Comparison of different sequencing depths in RNAseq.
  • Development of an ATACseq pipeline to run using Snakemake. The code can be visualized here (still ongoing).
  • Development of a ShinyApp to allow wetlab members to visualize the omics produced in the lab in an easy way. The code can be visualized here.
  • Analysis of transcriptomics (RNAseq), cistromics and epigenomics (ChIP-seq), and DNA accessibility data (ATACseq).
