Vavouri’s lab, Institut Josep Carreras

August 2021 - Present
Bioinformatician & master’s thesis student

Under the supervision of Dr. Tanya Vavouri, I am studying the PIWI-interacting RNAs (piRNAs) and their biogenesis. In January of 2022, I completed my master’s thesis (MSc in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics, UOC) with honors, where I analyzed the piRNA variation across closely related species and the factors influencing this variation.

My tasks in this position have been::
  • Development of a pipeline to pre-process small RNA-seq data with focus to the analysis of piRNAs.
  • Development of an approach for differential expression analysis without gene length differences when comparing multiple species.
  • Mentoring of bachelor’s and master’s thesis students.

Master’s thesis:
  • Analysis of the variation in piRNA expression across three Mus species.
