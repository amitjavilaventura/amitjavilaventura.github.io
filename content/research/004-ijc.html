Vavouri’s lab, Institut Josep Carreras

August 2021 - Present
Bioinformatician & Master’s thesis student

Under the supervision of Dr. Tanya Vavouri, I am studying the PIWI-interacting RNAs (piRNAs) and their biogenesis. I have recently completed a master’s thesis (MSc in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics) with honors, where I analyzed the piRNA variation across closely related species and the factors influencing this variation.

My contributions to this research have been:
  • Analysis of smallRNA-seq data.
  • Retrieve and analyze multiple-sequence alignments using ENSEMBL Compara Perl API.
  • Develop an approach for differential expression analysis without gene length differences when comparing multiple species.

Master’s thesis:
  • Analysis of the variation in piRNA expression across three Mus species.
